Kellye's Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 4 slices of Cheese
    • Cheddar
    • Mozarella
    • Pepper jack
  • 1 tsp of salt

STEPS

  1. Spread butter on bread and pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Cover with second slide of bread
  4. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
